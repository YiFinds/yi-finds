Our Story

Before creating Yi Finds, our founder worked with international customers and often introduced them to products from Taiwan.

Many of them enjoyed these products so much that they later asked how they could get the same items from abroad. That experience became the idea behind Yi Finds.

Today, Yi Finds exists to present carefully selected Taiwanese products to international consumers and make them easier to discover, understand, and enjoy around the world.
