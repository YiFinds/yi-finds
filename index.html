Our Story

Yi Finds is inspired by 宜奕工作室, keeping the original Taiwanese identity while creating a simple English brand for international consumers.

The name Yi Finds reflects our mission: to find, select, and share products from Taiwan that deserve to be discovered by the world.

From small makers to modern brands, our goal is to help more people experience Taiwan through the products they use, taste, and gift.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Yi Finds.

What is Yi Finds?

Yi Finds is a brand created to introduce carefully selected Taiwanese products to global consumers.

Do you sell products directly?

The website can be used first as a landing page. Later, it can be expanded into a full online store with product pages, payments, and shipping options.

What kind of products do you focus on?

We focus on Taiwanese products with quality, story, and international appeal, including food, tea, lifestyle goods, design objects, gifts, beauty, and wellness products.

Can Taiwanese brands work with Yi Finds?

Yes. Taiwanese brands and makers can contact us to explore international presentation and distribution opportunities.

Is Yi Finds connected to 宜奕工作室?

Yes. Yi Finds keeps the identity of 宜奕工作室 while using an English name that is easier for global customers to remember.
